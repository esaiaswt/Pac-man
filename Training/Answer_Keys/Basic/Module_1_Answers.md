## Part 2: Browser DevTools Exploration

**Task 3**:
- Elements Tab: Shows HTML structure of the page
- Console Tab: May show messages or be empty
- Sources Tab: Should show 5 .js files (audio, game, maze, pacman, ghost)

**Task 4**:
1. `console.log("Hello, Pac-Man!")` → Displays: Hello, Pac-Man!
